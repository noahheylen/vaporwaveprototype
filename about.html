The 'Death of Slaughterhouses Archive' is the worlds largest digital archive related to the demise of slaughterhouses.
Founded and funded by Alep Meats, the world leader in lab grown meat, the archive launched february 28 2194
in celebration of the 75 years existence of the global ban on meat consumption.
The archive is categorized in different collections and open for everybody.
We continually update this website as we gather and process material.
